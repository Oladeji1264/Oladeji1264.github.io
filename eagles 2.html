Our School Vision
The creation of our schools Eagles' mind is to encourage learning and development for children to believe in themselves, and become independent learners through creative curriculum activities. To ensure total development of each Child and establish value that will allow children to act with thoughtfulness and humility.

Core Value
Responsibility, Integrity,Compassion, Morals, Services and Reward.
